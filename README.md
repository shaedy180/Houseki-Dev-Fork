Houseki is a mod that is based on the realism of stones/rocks, gems, crystals, and metals, and tries to replicate the real-life counterparts of the items added! The mod features lots of new ores, tools, armors, and full armor effects!

---
## Don't see the item you want in the mod?
Check out the [Suggestion Box](https://whispermeter.com/feedback/housekidev) and suggest what you want! Or just leave feedback, up to you!

**!!Remember, your suggestion must be a real thing and have real uses!!**

You can also look at the [Development Board](https://trello.com/b/gkn3qGlL/houseki-development), and you can see if your suggestion is being worked on or not!

## ⚒️ New Crafters!

### Crusher
![Crusher](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/6330ccaa0c2e6cd1b91ee1fbbdcd2b233f955d74.png)

The Crusher is a new block entity that crushes minerals to make other minerals!

### Foundry
![Foundry](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1e4632d6-fe17-48f5-bbfd-7a9a25801123)

The Foundry melts certain metals and then pours them into casts to produce tools and armor components!

# 🗿 Rock Deposits

### 🪨 Limestone</summary>

![Limestone](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/eec1cf3b607dbbdfa5212f43f6049410b4148a2e.png)

Limestone is a sedimentary rock composed mainly of calcium carbonate, typically in the form of calcite or aragonite. It forms from the accumulation of marine organisms' remains and can contain other minerals like magnesium carbonate and clay.

Limestone can be crafted into a variety of building blocks as well!

![Limestone Blocks](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/0feafd803de8c501d6a64612bbbc6725628589c8.png)

### 🪨 Slate

![Slate](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/ed42c08a831b97b47b0851d039609afb354650b6_0.webp)

Slate is a fine-grained, foliated, homogeneous, metamorphic rock derived from an original shale-type sedimentary rock composed of clay or volcanic ash through low-grade, regional metamorphism.

Slate can be crafted into a variety of building blocks as well!

![Slate blocks](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/7a91d0788232790786696861b4dde4bc58929f94.png)

### 🪨 Bauxite

![Bauxite](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/7f9686854a5ce7514e564d783d197fb015d0708a.png)

Bauxite is a sedimentary rock and the primary source of aluminum, composed mainly of aluminum hydroxide minerals like gibbsite, boehmite, and diaspore.

# 🧙‍♀️ Trim Effects & Armor Effects

Houseki features full armor effects and some trim effects!
This means when you wear a full set of certain armors in the mod you will gain a special effect that matches the real world uses.

Same with the trim effects! Read more on the Wiki!
